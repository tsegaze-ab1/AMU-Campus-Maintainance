About Platform

Why this system?

The Campus Maintenance Management System is a web-based platform designed to help universities manage maintenance activities in a modern and organized way.

In large campuses, many problems such as electrical failures, water issues, classroom damage, and equipment repair requests occur frequently. In traditional systems, these problems are reported manually using paper forms or verbal communication, which often leads to delays, lost requests, and poor coordination between departments.

This system provides a centralized digital solution where students, teachers, and staff can submit maintenance requests online through a simple interface. Once a request is submitted, the administrator can review the problem, assign the appropriate technician, and monitor the repair progress in real time.

Technicians can update the status of their work, and users can track their request without needing to visit the maintenance office. All requests are stored in the system, making the process more organized, transparent, and efficient.

By using this system, the university can reduce response time, improve communication, and maintain campus facilities in a more reliable and professional way.
